About (FOMAS) Friends of Manteca Animal Shelter

(FOMAS) Friends of Manteca Animal Shelter is a unique, non-profit organization located in Manteca, CA. We work in partnership with the Manteca Animal Shelter to support animals and the people who love them through successful rehoming, assistance with Spay/Neuter for those who qualify, and education.

Our mission is to make a positive impact on the lives of animals in our community through compassionate care, successful rehoming, and educational outreach programs.

How It Works

1

Fill out the DDO form

2

Visit Manteca Animal Shelter

3

Choose a dog and take them out

4

Return by 3:30 PM same day

📞 Contact for Info

Requirements: Must be 18+ years old with a valid driver's license or identification. Dogs must be returned by 3:30 PM unless special arrangements are made. Our DDO program allows for overnight stays.
