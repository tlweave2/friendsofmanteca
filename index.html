About Friends of Manteca Animal Shelter

Friends of Manteca Animal Shelter is a unique, non-profit animal-rescue organization located in Manteca, CA. We work in partnership with the Manteca Animal Shelter to support animals and the people who love them through rescue, rehoming, spay/neuter programs, and education.

Our mission is to make a positive impact on the lives of animals in our community through compassionate care, responsible rehoming, and educational outreach programs.
