About Us

Dedicated to supporting animals and the people who love them through rescue, education, and community outreach.

Our Story

Friends of Manteca Animal Shelter is a volunteer-led 501(c)(3) non-profit animal rescue organization located in Manteca, California. We work in partnership with the Manteca Animal Shelter to support animals and the people who love them through rescue, rehoming, spay/neuter programs, and education.

As a foster-based rescue, our animals are not kept in a traditional shelter environment but are instead cared for in the homes of our dedicated foster families. This approach allows us to provide personalized care and attention to each animal, helping them prepare for their forever homes while learning important socialization skills.

We are entirely funded by the generous support of our donors and the dedication of our volunteers. Every dollar donated and every hour volunteered goes directly toward helping animals in need find loving homes and receive the care they deserve.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

Our mission is to support animals and the people who love them through rescue, rehoming, spay/neuter programs, and education. We are dedicated to making a positive impact on the lives of animals in our community through compassionate care, responsible rehoming, and educational outreach programs.

We work tirelessly to find appropriate forever homes for animals in our care, provide vital medical services, promote responsible pet ownership, and advocate for animal welfare within our community.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion: We treat all animals with kindness, respect, and dignity.
  • Integrity: We maintain the highest ethical standards in all aspects of our work.
  • Dedication: We are committed to the well-being of every animal in our care.
  • Education: We believe in empowering our community through knowledge about animal welfare.
  • Collaboration: We partner with individuals and organizations to maximize our impact.
  • Transparency: We are accountable to our supporters and the community we serve.

What We Do

🏠

Animal Rescue & Adoption

We rescue animals from the shelter and place them in loving foster homes until they find their forever families.

✂️

Spay & Neuter Programs

We provide affordable spay and neuter services to help reduce pet overpopulation in our community.

🏥

Medical Care

We ensure all animals receive necessary medical care, vaccines, and treatment before adoption.

🎓

Education & Outreach

We educate the community about responsible pet ownership and animal welfare issues.

🐕

Doggy Day Out

Our program allows community members to take shelter dogs out for adventures, reducing stress and increasing adoption opportunities.

👥

Foster Program

We provide support and resources to foster families who open their homes to animals in need.
