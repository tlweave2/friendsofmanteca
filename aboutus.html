About Us

Our Story

Friends of Manteca Animal Shelter was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate animal lovers who saw a need to support the Manteca Animal Shelter and enhance the lives of homeless animals in our community. What began as a small group of volunteers has grown into a thriving non-profit organization dedicated to animal welfare.

Our organization works in partnership with the Manteca Animal Shelter to provide additional resources, support, and care for animals in need. We are committed to reducing the number of homeless animals in our community through adoption, education, and spay/neuter programs.

As a foster-based rescue, our animals are not kept in a shelter environment but are instead cared for in the homes of our dedicated foster families. This allows us to provide personalized care and attention to each animal, helping them prepare for their forever homes while learning important socialization skills.

Over the years, we have helped hundreds of dogs and cats find loving homes, provided essential medical care to animals in need, and implemented community programs to improve the lives of animals throughout Manteca and surrounding areas.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support animals and the people who love them through rescue, rehoming, spay/neuter, and education. We are dedicated to making a difference, one animal at a time.

We work tirelessly to find appropriate forever homes for animals in our care, provide vital medical services, promote responsible pet ownership, and advocate for animal welfare within our community.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion: We treat all animals with kindness, respect, and dignity.
  • Integrity: We maintain the highest ethical standards in all aspects of our work.
  • Dedication: We are committed to the well-being of every animal in our care.
  • Education: We believe in empowering our community through knowledge about animal welfare.
  • Collaboration: We partner with individuals, organizations, and businesses to maximize our impact.
  • Transparency: We are accountable to our supporters and the community we serve.

Our History

2015

Foundation

Friends of Manteca Animal Shelter was founded by a group of dedicated volunteers committed to supporting the local animal shelter.

2016

501(c)(3) Status

We obtained our official non-profit status, allowing us to expand our fundraising efforts and community programs.

2018

Foster Program Launch

We established our foster program, moving away from a traditional shelter environment to provide more personalized care for our animals.

2020

Spay/Neuter Initiative

We launched our community spay/neuter program, providing affordable services to reduce pet overpopulation in Manteca.

2022

Community Education Program

We expanded our educational outreach, offering workshops and resources to promote responsible pet ownership.

2024

New Partnerships

We established collaborations with local businesses and organizations to increase our impact and reach within the community.
