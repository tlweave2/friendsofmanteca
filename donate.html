Donations

Your support makes it possible for us to make a difference, one animal at a time.

(FOMAS) Friends of Manteca Animal Shelter is a volunteer led 501(c)(3) non-profit that is funded entirely by the financial support of our generous donors and the dedication of time from our committed volunteers. With your support we can provide more services to shelter animals in Manteca. Often animals come into shelters with conditions the facility is not equipped to address. Our mission is in working toward providing those much-needed services allowing for successful rehoming. Additionally reducing the pet overpopulation through our assistance with affordable Spay & Neuter program, and work with severely injured and ill animals who are out of options through our emergency medical care programs.

We are so thankful to our incredible community that has come together year after year to make it possible for us to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need. We could not do it without your support. Thank you!
