Donations

Your support makes it possible for us to make a difference, one animal at a time.

Friends of Manteca Animal Shelter is a volunteer led 501(c)(3) non-profit animal rescue that is entirely funded by the financial support of our generous donors and the dedication of time from our volunteers. With your support we are able to rescue more animals, work toward reducing the pet overpopulation problem through our Affordable Spay & Neuter program, and work with severely injured and ill animals who are out of options through our emergency medical care programs.

We are so thankful to our incredible community that has come together year after year to make it possible for us to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need. We could not do it without your support. Thank you!
