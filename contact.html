Frequently Asked Questions

How do I adopt an animal? +

Visit our Animals page to view available pets, fill out an adoption application, and schedule a meet-and-greet. Our adoption team will guide you through the process to ensure a perfect match.

What are your adoption fees? +

Adoption fees vary by animal type and age. Dogs are typically $150-$250, cats $75-$125. All animals are spayed/neutered, vaccinated, and microchipped. Visit our Adoption Fees page for detailed information.

How can I volunteer? +

We welcome volunteers! Fill out our volunteer application and attend an orientation session. Opportunities include dog walking, cat socialization, event assistance, and administrative tasks.

Do you accept animal surrenders? +

We work with the Manteca Animal Shelter for surrenders. Please contact us first to discuss your situation - we may be able to provide resources to help you keep your pet or assist with rehoming.

What donations do you need most? +

We always need blankets, towels, pet food, toys, and cleaning supplies. Monetary donations help us provide medical care and spay/neuter services. Check our current wishlist on our Donate page.
